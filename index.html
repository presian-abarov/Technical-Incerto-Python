Technical-Incerto-Python

The Technical-Incerto-Python project aims to provide a comprehensive resource for the study and implementation of statistical concepts discussed in Nassim Taleb's book "Statistical Consequences of Fat Tails." By reproducing numerical examples, figures, and delving into the mathematical details, this project serves as an invaluable companion for researchers, practitioners, and enthusiasts interested in understanding and applying the theories presented in the book. Additionally, the project offers a collection of reusable Python functions in statistics that are based on the concepts from the book, making it easier for users to integrate these statistical methods into their work.
